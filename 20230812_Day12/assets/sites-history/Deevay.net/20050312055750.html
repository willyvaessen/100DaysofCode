My new website
geschreven door Willy Vaessen   
Wednesday 09 March 2005
Well,

I seem to have finally found something I really like.
As you can see, this website is powered by Mambo.
They have created a CMS (Content Management System) with lot of things to change and configure, giving me enough options to spend time on.

I hope you like it. If you do, please check out the poll on your right hand. If you don't like my website, or the design, you may use the poll as well.
However, since I am very happy with this layout and the system behind all this, I will probably not change it too soon.

I just might decide to add a Dutch section, but only if I have enough time to translate all pages and templates, which is bound to be a lot of work.

A few nice features of this system are:
- possibility to register and log in to a separate part of the website
- RSS-feeds
- easy way to post news items.

Like I said, there is a lot for me to find out about this system. Expect some changes in the near future, and also quite some news-items.

Enjoy your stay ;)
